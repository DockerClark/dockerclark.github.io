About Me

Welcome to my academic website! I am an assistant research scientist in Dr. Hayek's lab at NYU's ES Department specializing in data-focused animal agriculture research. My research currently focuses on evaluating the real costs associated with american beef production.

I completed my BA in Environmental Studies from NYU in 2022, where I worked on soil heavy-metal contamination and phytoremediation. Since, I have been TA-ing and researching in the Environmental Studies department.

My current research interests include regenerative animal agriculture, nature-based climate solutions, food systems, and plant-people interations. I will be applying for PhD candidacy in the above areas (and more) this coming fall (2025).

Research

My research program focuses on animal agriculture. Currently, I am working on a couple projects including:

  • RegenAg: An evaluation of regenerative agriculture as a climate mitigation strategy (Lit review and meta analysis).
  • True Cost of Beef: Incorporating postive and negative externalities and direct and indirect subsidies in the the retail price of US beef.
